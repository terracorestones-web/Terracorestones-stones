How We Work

Your Operational Presence in India

You own the deal with your supplier. We own everything that happens on the ground between the quarry and your container.

01
SUPPLIER & QUARRY AUDITS
We vet quarries and factories against your spec, and inspect raw blocks for hidden flaws before a single cut is made.
02
PRODUCTION QA
On-ground during your run — enforcing dimensional tolerances and confirming shade and vein consistency via dry-lays.
03
CONTAINER SIGN-OFF
No container is sealed without our personal inspection — packing, lashing, and aesthetic uniformity, verified.
04
EXPORT LOGISTICS
Documentation, ISPM 15 compliance, and port coordination — from the factory floor to your destination port.

About Terracore

Boots on the Ground.
Working for the Buyer.

Terracore Stones is not a trader and not a middleman. We're engaged directly by international buyers to act as their own presence inside India — paid for consultancy, QA, and logistics, never for the material.

Founded by Kush Sharma, who has spent over 15 years embedded in India's natural stone trade, sourcing across the country's major quarry belts — supporting architects, designers, and developers in India, Mauritius, and now expanding to Australia and the UAE.
