About Terracore

Where Earth Becomes
Art

Terracore Stones is a premium natural stone atelier sourcing the finest sandstones, limestones, and marbles directly from Rajasthan and across India's most storied geological regions.

We work alongside architects and interior designers globally — from concept to execution — delivering customised stone solutions for pools, wall cladding, artistic panels, flooring, and statement tabletops across India and Mauritius.

CONSULTANT · TERRACORE STONES

The Craftsman

15 Years of Stone.
One Standard: Excellence.

Kush Sharma brings over 15 years of experience in the natural stone trade across India, with deep expertise in sourcing from regions such as Rajasthan. Through Terracore India, he focuses on high-quality sandstones, limestones, and marbles.

His work spans both residential and commercial projects, delivering customised stone solutions for pools, wall cladding, artistic panels, flooring, and tabletops.

He works closely with architects and interior designers globally, supporting projects from concept to execution. His portfolio includes completed work across India and Mauritius.
